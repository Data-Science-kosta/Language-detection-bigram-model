Brian Clegg (born 12 April 1930) is a former Australian rules footballer who played with South Melbourne in the Victorian Football League (VFL).Clegg made two appearances, as a half back flanker, in the 1951 VFL season. He played those games beside his brother, Brownlow Medal winner Ron Clegg.He later made a name for himself in the Ballarat Football League, where he played for Geelong West. In 1956 he won the league's best and fairest award.
