Exeter is an Amtrak train station in Exeter, New Hampshire. The station is a sheltered platform that replaced a station built in 1891.The station is served five times daily by Amtrak's Downeaster service. On average, 230 passengers board or detrain daily, making it the busiest stop in New Hampshire.The station is located next to the Pan Am Railways mainline, formerly the Western Route mainline of the Boston & Maine Railroad.
