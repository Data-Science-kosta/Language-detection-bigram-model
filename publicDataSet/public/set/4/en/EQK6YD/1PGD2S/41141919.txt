Hend Khaleh (Persian: هندخاله‎, also Romanized as Hend Khāleh; also known as Hendeh Khāleh, Hendūkhāleh, Hind Khāleh, and Khind-Khale) is a village in Hend Khaleh Rural District, Tulem District, Sowme'eh Sara County, Gilan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 2,188, in 613 families.
